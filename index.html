About us

IEEE is a leader in engineering and technology education, providing resources for pre-university, university, and continuing professional education. IEEE offers innovative STEM and university education and recognition programs for students and their teachers, facilitates the accreditation of engineering programs at the university level, and offers ongoing continuing professional education for practitioners and engineering faculty through platforms such as the IEEE Learning Network (ILN).

In 1976, the Bombay Section was formed with a Territory of Maharashtra,Goa, Gujarat (which later separated to form a Section itself on 15 th August 1990), Madhya Pradesh (which now includes newly formed Chhatisgarh) and Diu & Daman UT (Except Pune Metropolitan City which is separate Section from 26th June 2010), closely followed by Bangalore & Delhi.

IEEE Bombay Section Educational Activities is a part of the IEEE Bombay Section with a focus on conducting various programs for the students and Faculty members for the improvement of their skills and knowledge.

Mission: Serve the IEEE community and fulfil the goal of Advancing Technology for Humanity.

Vision: Education is your Best Friend. It stays with you in good and bad times equally.
